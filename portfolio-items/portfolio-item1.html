Coupons Market

An Online Store

Who doesn't love sales?! If only there was a way to get discounts on everything you want to buy all the time... Oh wait.

This project was one of my firsts big projects that I programmed and came through a few phases. It started as a deep backend system based of generics and SQL connections and became a Springboot monster with different repositories, services, WEB and REST connections and a bunch of JWT security.

Although the website's frontend hasn't made its final form yet, I am deeply proud of this complex backend system right there. But stay tuned for a full operating website!
